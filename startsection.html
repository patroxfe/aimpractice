How to play

Reflex - A reliable script checks your reaction time, which translates into a scale: 0-220ms 221-400ms 401ms+

Aim Practice - It consists in clicking the pink dots as quickly and efficiently as possible. My system calculates your points based on the time it takes to hit the next dot and your accuracy.
